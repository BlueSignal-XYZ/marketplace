# BlueSignal-XYZ — Overview

## Mission

Open-source water quality monitoring infrastructure. BlueSignal builds hardware solutions for environmental monitoring. The marketplace platform operates under a separate brand.

## Brand Architecture

| Brand | Domain | Scope | Identity |
|-------|--------|-------|----------|
| **BlueSignal** | bluesignal.xyz | Hardware — WQM-1 sensors, monitoring devices, cloud dashboards | Hardware brand |
| **WaterQuality.Trading** | waterquality.trading | Marketplace — nutrient credit trading, deregulated water credits | Marketplace/credits brand |
| **Aquaria.world** | aquaria.world | Aquaculture dealer-installer network, channel strategy | Consulting/advisory |

**CRITICAL:** Never blur these brands. BlueSignal = hardware. WaterQuality.Trading = marketplace/credits. Aquaria = aquaculture network. Each has its own email identity and context. See [[Knowledge/People/Team/Founder]] for email routing rules.

## Focus Areas

- **IoT / Environmental Monitoring** — Distributed sensor networks for water quality measurement
- **Blockchain** — On-chain verification for nutrient credit transactions (Polygon network)
- **Real-Time Data** — Live sensor feeds, dashboards, and alerting
- **Open Source** — Hardware designs and firmware are open-source
